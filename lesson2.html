Using Social Media




One of Abena's favorite hobbies is growing flowers! However, no one she knows likes to grow flowers. She plans to download Instagram to upload photos of her flowers and join the flower-growing community!

There are all types of communities on social media apps like Instagram, Tiktok, and Twitter! You can connect with others and find people with the same interests as you.

First, to get Instagram, Abena has to download it from the app store, because she has an Apple phone.

Question: Which app should Abena use?


Correct! Next, to create a post, Abena has to click on the plus button. She will then have to pick if she wants to share a post (picture), a story (a post that is there for 24 hours), a reel (video), or start a live stream. In Abena's case, she is going to share a post.

Then, Abena needs to select what photo(s) she would like to post, she chooses the picture of her flowers. After that, press next.

Abena can now customize her post by adding a description, setting the location, tagging people, or adding music. When creating a description you can use hashtags. Hashtags are words or numbers that come after a # sign. Use relevant hashtags to your post to make your post more discoverable.

Question: Which of these hashtags should Abena use?



Wow! Abena's post is doing great, she finally can meet people with the same interests as her! Social media is a great tool to find your community and new friends.

Although, it is important to have online safety when using these apps which is discussed in another lesson.
